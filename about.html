STEM yearly innovation computing and conference

Last year's VEX Robotics tournament featured five teams, and it was an incredible and fierce competition. We are really proud of the effort, enthusiasm, and dedication that these students put into this competition. Isn't it great that the winner and first runner-up are both female? Absolutely!! It demonstrates that if girls are given the chance, opportunity, and platform to learn and explore areas that society labels "difficult," they can compete and win. We're quite proud of our young girls. The males rose to the occasion, but they were no match for the girls. We've learned that learning can be enjoyable and interactive as a result of all of this, and we're delighted to provide the platform for these students. Are we prepared for the coming year?
