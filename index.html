STEM for us by us

It is with great pleasure that we invite you to the 2nd STEM in Education Conference. This conference is a biennial event hosted by Africa Girls Empathy. In 2022, it will be stream live on all Africa Girls Empathy social media pages. The theme for STEM 2022 is “STEM For Us By Us“.

2022.11.25(FRI) ~ 26(SAT)

@ Radisson Blu conference hall, Aberdeen Freetown.

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various tech background, they will talk about the important of STEM and innovation.

Exhibition

Appreciate various creation, created by the innovation groups to show case. vote for your favorite project

Forum

Have your time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts from each topic of discussion during the lectures.

Ignite

Get an opportunity to network with innovators and tech experts from various tech backgrounds
